## A "Learn Everything, Give Up Halfway" Kind of Guy 🛠️

Welcome to my chaotic lab! I’m someone who gets easily fascinated by almost anything. I dive headfirst into new hobbies and technologies, though... I admittedly tend to lose steam and abandon them halfway through when the next shiny thing catches my eye.

But hey, **giving up halfway just means I got to taste twice as many cool things!** 🤷‍♂️

### 🌀 My Typical Project Lifecycle
*   **0 ~ 10%**: "OMG, this is amazing! I must master this immediately!" (Hyper-focused)
*   **10 ~ 50%**: Staying up late, tinkering, setting up environments, building a mini-prototype.
*   **50%... 📈**: "Oh look, a butterfly!" (Instantly distracted by a new project)

### 🎒 My Collection of Half-Mastered Skills & Interests
*   **Design & Observation** | Exploring **Service Design**, mapping touchpoints, and figuring out how people interact with systems.
*   **Creative Suite** | Messing around with Blender (3D), After Effects (Motion Graphics), and DaVinci Resolve.
*   **Tech & Hardware Gear** | PC building, custom keyboard layouts, and diving into the audiophile rabbit hole (DACs, Amps, and IEMs).
*   **Gaming & Automation** | Spending way too many hours on *Factorio* and technical *Minecraft* modpacks (AE2, Mekanism).

> 💡 *"Life is too short to only do one thing. I’d rather be a happy beginner in ten different fields than a bored master in one."*
